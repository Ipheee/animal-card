Golden Retriever

The Golden Retriever is a medium-sized breed with a striking double coat. They have a broad head and a strong muzzle. Their eyes are typically dark, and their ears are medium-sized and drooping. Their legs are straight with powerful hind legs and muscular thighs.
The Golden Retriever is a clever and intelligent breed. They are eager to please, calm, and even-tempered, have a stellar sense of smell, and love playing fetch. Their gentle nature makes their training an easy process and loving them easier.

  • Height: 20-22 inches in females; 22-24 inches in males
  • Weight: 25-32 kilograms for females; 30-34 kilograms for males
  • Lifespan: 10-12 years
  • Coat Type: Dense and double-coat
  • Colors: Cream and all shades of golden

Fun Fact: Golden Retrievers instinctively love to swim. They're always up for a game of fetch in the lake.
